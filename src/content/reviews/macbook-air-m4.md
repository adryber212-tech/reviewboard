The MacBook Air M4 isn't trying to reinvent the laptop. It's trying to perfect it — and it comes closer than any laptop before it.

## Performance

The M4 chip brings a 10-core CPU and 10-core GPU. For daily tasks — browsing, email, coding, light video editing — this thing flies. It handles 4K video playback without breaking a sweat. The fanless design means zero noise, always.

## Display & Design

The 13.6-inch Liquid Retina display hits 500 nits and supports P3 wide color. It's bright, sharp, and a joy to use. The notch is still there, but you stop noticing after day one. At 2.7 pounds, it disappears in your bag.

## Battery Life

Apple claims 18 hours. In real-world testing with mixed use (browsing, YouTube, Slack, VS Code), I got about 16 hours. That's exceptional.

## The Bottom Line

The MacBook Air M4 is the best laptop for most people. It's powerful enough for 90% of users, lasts all day, and costs $1,099. If you don't need a Pro machine, this is it.
