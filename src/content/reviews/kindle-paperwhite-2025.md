The Kindle Paperwhite is the Goldilocks of e-readers. Not too big, not too small, not too expensive. After a month of daily use, here's my full review.

## Display

The 7-inch, 300 PPI display is crisp and clear. Text looks like printed paper. The adjustable warm light lets you shift from cool blue to warm amber — great for nighttime reading without eye strain.

## Battery Life

Amazon says "up to 10 weeks." With 30 minutes of reading per day, I got about 8 weeks before needing a charge. Still outstanding.

## Reading Experience

This is where Kindle shines. The e-ink display is easy on the eyes, the page turns are snappy enough, and the built-in dictionary, highlights, and notes work flawlessly.

## What's Missing

No Audible support, no expandable storage, and Amazon's ecosystem is restrictive. If you buy into Kindle, you're buying into Amazon for good.

## Final Verdict

If you read books, buy a Kindle Paperwhite. It's the best e-reader for the money. Period.
